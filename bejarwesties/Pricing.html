Pricing and other information

Breeding Criteria

Our breeding criteria is simple - do what's best for the Mother and pups. We breed healthy and strong parents so that their puppies will be just as healthy and strong.

Health Screening

Our dogs visit the vets regularly throughout their first 8 weeks of life. They recieve vaccines x, y, and z as well as a physical exam of health from a certified vet.
