Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to making the adoption experience of a westie puppy simple, easy and transparent. The website will inform possible customers about litters, prices and our contact information. The website has to be extremely user friendly and puppy-themed.

Audience

My target audience are perspective dog owners! That could be young families, older couples, or young, single adults! Most reside within Colorado and/or the United States.
